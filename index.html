About Us

Inspiration

The inspiration for RandomActs came to us by witnessing people brightening other peoples days with small acts. So we built an app that helps you reach out and show kindness to those around you.

Empowerment

RandomActs will give you a simple task to reach out and spread kindness to others, we pick one of your uploaded contacts at random to reach out and help make their day with a simple act of kindness.

Improvement

RandomActs goal is to improve the lives of you and those around you, Research shows that kind acts release serotonin, slow aging, and reduce stress levels when carried out.
